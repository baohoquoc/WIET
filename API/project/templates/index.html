Recommended dishes based on your hobbies.

Our recommendation system will give you the most accurate food suggestions.

  • Recommend dishes near you.
  • Recommend dishes for vegetarian.
  • Remove allergy ingredients from your dishes.
  • Recommend from huge amounts of data.

This feature will learn from what you manipulate in the application, which will gradually give more accurate suggestions. Besides, it also has a recommend algorithm for people who have similar hobbies to you.

Discover our vast amount of data.

Data is the most important thing for a food application.

With the data throughout Vietnam, including vegetarian and savory dishes, prices, and restaurant addresses. You can easily find information about restaurants and dishes faster than ever before.

You can search for your favorite food by searching for the name of the dish or the name of the restaurant. The application will learn over time and offer your favorite food.

Detailed information about the dish.

This is the place to include your food, including a number of features.

  • Dish detail information.
  • Order feature.
  • Other dishes from this restaurant.
  • The distance from the restaurant to your current location.
  • Bookmark feature.

Easily manage your personal data.

The data you provide us is used to give accurate suggestions for your dish.

We will collect some of your basic data such as your favorite food, where you are living, your date of birth, whether you are a vegetarian or not, and your allergy information. From there give personal recommendations.

  • Automatically retrieve your location data.
  • Automatically retrieve your name and avatar data.
  • Other data will be collected manually.
